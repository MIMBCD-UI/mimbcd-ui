MIMBCD-UI

Medical Imaging Multimodality Breast Cancer Diagnosis User Interface (MIMBCD-UI) Project
involves the collaborative effort of two Portuguese Research Institutions: INESC-ID and ISR/IST.

  • Discover

About

The project is being developed by the Associated Laboratory - Institute for Systems and Robotics (ISR). This project proposes the development of a methodology for detection and cancer targeting breast using multimodality medical imaging and textual information.
